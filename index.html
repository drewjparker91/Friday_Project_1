About Me

Drew Parker is currently an Assistant Lacrosse Coach and Summer Camp Director at Oregon Episcopal School where he is in charge of over 20 councelors and the well-being of more than 350 children ages 6-13. Drew is driven by the gratitude he feels by helping his student-athletes grow and develop both on and off the field over their high school careers. In 2019, Drew was Reconized by the Oregon High School Lacrosse Association as the Assistant Coach of the Year. During college, Drew spent two years in marketing while working for Rhino Lacrosse, and after college worked for two years as a personal assistant to the CEO of a Ed Catalyst Group, a company that works to integrate technology into education.

Currently, Drew is enrolled at Epicodus, a Portland based coding "bootcamp", taking their "Ruby and React" program. He will learn JavaScript, Ruby, and React during his time at Epicodus. After school, he hopes to work for Salesforce. If you have any questions for Drew, please e-mail him at drewjparker91@gmail.com.
